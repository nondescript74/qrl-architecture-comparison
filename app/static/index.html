Why the outputs diverge
Markets behave like a fog of noise: asynchronous, overlapping, partially conflicting signals. The left side emits one answer at a time. The right side maintains multiple live paths, updates them with new evidence, and delays commitment until the structure becomes clearer. That is the core difference between prompt-level inference and tracked state estimation.
